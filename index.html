Get Started

You're on the bus during rush-hour and bam! an idea hits you. You've got seconds to jot it down before the cacophony of the bus distracts you and your idea slips away. We're here for you: ready to hold onto your tidbits of inspiration and saving them for a time that works for you. No more staring at the ceiling being frustration with slippery genius.

Features

Add ideas anytime, anywhere.

Even when you aren't online, you can add ideas to your account. Your account will sync next time you connect to the internet.

Available on all devices

Not only will your notes sync with your online account, they'll sync will all of your devices whether they're Android or Ubuntu.

As much space as you need.

Have a million ideas? We've got space for that. Let your imagination sore and jot every little piece down!

Time travel (sort of)

We store all changes you make on our servers so that you can jump back however far you need to recover versions of your entries.

Like visuals?

Add attachments, links, and upload photos to accompany thoughts or as stand-alone pieces of inspiration.

Organize your thoughts your way

A brain can be a messy and complicated place, but your account doesn't need to be. You can organize the way you want to using lists, notes, colour codes, tags, #hashtags, you name it!.

Searchable ideas

Remember that one time you did that thing at that place with, you know, that person? Can't put your finger on your idea? That's okay! Our search function will allow you to narrow down the search and find what your looking for.

Present your ideas

When it comes to presentations, we've got your back. Our display features will make bringing your ideas together easier than ever before.

Share and collaborate

Share your thoughts with friends on Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr, and by email. Or if they're also members create a group project where all your ideas can come together.
